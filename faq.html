Who can become a Campus Ambassador? + -

Any student currently enrolled in a recognized college can apply.

How do I know if I got selected as a Campus Ambassador? + -

You will be notified via email or WhatsApp upon selection.

What will be my responsibility as a Campus Ambassador for Pravaah? + -

Your main role is to promote Pravaah events and represent your campus.

How will I contribute to Pravaah as a Campus Ambassador? + -

You will help with outreach, promotions, and student engagement.

What incentives do I receive as a Campus Ambassador? + -

Certificates, badges, networking opportunities, and rewards.
